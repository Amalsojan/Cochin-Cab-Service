ABOUT US


Cochin Cab is India’s largest mobility platform and one of the world’s largest ride-hailing companies, serving 250+ cities across India, Australia, New Zealand, and the UK. The C-Cab app offers mobility solutions by connecting customers to drivers and a wide range of vehicles across bikes, auto-rickshaws, metered taxis, and cabs, enabling convenience and transparency for hundreds of millions of consumers and over 1.5 million driver-partners.

Cochin Cab’s core mobility offering in India is supplemented by its electric-vehicle arm, C-Cab Electric; India’s largest fleet management business, Cochin Cab Fleet Technologies and C-Cab Skilling, that aims to enable millions of livelihood opportunities for India's youth. With its acquisition of Ridlr, India’s leading public transportation app and investment in Vogo, a dockless scooter sharing solution, Cochin Cab is looking to build mobility for the next billion Indians. Cochin-Cab also extends its consumer offerings like micro-insurance and credit led payments through C-Cab Financial Services and a range of owned food brands through India’s largest network of kitchens under its Food business.

Cochin-Cab was founded in Oct 2022 by Amal with a mission to build mobility for a billion people.
